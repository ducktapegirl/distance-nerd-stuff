Thursday, August 23rd
Workout: run Minutes: 46:33 Miles: 5.5
Comments: I was originally going to take today off, but Deanna gave me a call last night, so I agreed to meet her today. We met up around 4:00 at my house, and decided we'd shoot for 40-45 minutes. Deanna had never been into the canyon behind my Torrey Pines before, and I never go when I'm running on my own (it's totally safe, but a little isolated, so it spooks me), so that's where we went. We went a nice steady pace on the way out, then turned around at 22 minutes and got moving a little more, but it felt good. She also convinced me to run up "puke hill," which, in high school, we always had a nice system of running half and walking half, while timing half of the walking anyway, because it was that intense. I made it up almost the whole thing, minus like 10 feet, which is pretty decent for me right now. All in all it was a good run, much better than yesterday.
Extras: abs
Wednesday, August 22nd
Workout: run Minutes: 59:02 Miles: 7
Comments: Today was a slow moving day, both before and during the run. Woke up around 8:45, thinking I would drive over to the San Elijo Lagoon to run, but I was really craving a bowl of cereal, so of course I had to wait a little to run after eating that. Then I manage to lock my keys in the trunk outside my house, so I spent about 30 minutes breaking into my trunk, by which time it was around 10:00 and way too hot to run in the Lagoon. Not wanting to wait until the afternoon (I had lunch plans anyway), I decided to just drive to the beach again. Parked outside the north lot and walked, slowly, in, before finally getting going. Ran south to Guy Fleming and then back north to Powerhouse Park. The first 3 miles (heading south) were really sluggish and I considered cutting the run to 4 or 5 miles, but once I got onto the cliffs I felt a good bit better, and decided to do the full "long" run I had planned, which I'm glad I did.
