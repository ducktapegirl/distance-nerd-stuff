Season Recap
    Can't really say I was pleased with the season. No PR's, not even for specific courses. It was all a little frustrating; I still can't tell if it was mental or physical. I mean, I felt in shape, and I was hitting pretty solid workout times. I guess I just have to find the guts to race. I wonder a little about the training, putting in so many miles this summer only to drop to less than I was running last year. I don't think I can determine necessarily whether more miles is better or not (although I have my opinions), but the fact remains that I was halfway between two programs and that definitely will never work. Gotta get this straightened out with HBH.

10/10-10/16 Weekly Mileage: 13.5
Saturday, October 16th, Twin Brook Invite, Cumberland, ME
Distance: 6k Time: 25:11 Total Miles: 8.5
Comments:

   Argh! There is no good reason that people should get slower from year to year. This blows. I almost hesistate to write something so negative for fear of planting bad thoughts into my head, but hell, they're already in there so what harm can it do?
   Today was crap. Every mile felt progressively worse. I hung with Kristen for 2 miles as opposed to wimping out early, which is more than I've been doing, but I should have been up there the whole time. 41 seconds slower than last year. Yeah, on the one hand you can say time is meaningless in cross country, because every race is different and you're out there to race people, but then again... last year I was 26th as opposed to 42 today. Kristen was 24. I should have been right fucking there.
   There are a lot of things I could say, excuses I could make. "If only I didn't have a cold for the first half of the week, yadda yadda..." that's no good. You don't win races by predicting what would happen under ideal conditions. You either perform, or you don't. At NEWMACs and regionals, if I don't perform, that's it, that's the result, no hypothetical situations. I have to take what I have that day, and just fucking race, and that's what the team has to do to.

I know everyone has a bad season from time to time. I can't expect to improve eight years in a row. But I am simply not pulling. Geez, if I just had one sign to give me some confidence and tell me I can do this...
